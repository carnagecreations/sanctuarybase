Welcome to St. Francis Rescue & Sanctuary (SFR)

We are a grassroots, community-supported multi-species rescue shelter in sunny Yuma, Arizona. Our animals are cared for almost entirely by passionate volunteers like you. Here, you will be interacting with horses, dogs, and cats.

Our mission is to provide safety, nutrition, and peaceful rehabilitation to neglected, surrendered or elderly animals. Because of Yuma's intense summer climates, strict safety and meticulous hygiene are non-negotiable.

To make things easy, we have split our training into **10 Core Required Modules** (which you must complete to volunteer on-site) and **14 Advanced Enrichment Modules** (which are completely optional but earn you bonus XP and certifications)!
